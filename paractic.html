Protect Our Oceans

Explore the beauty beneath the surface — and the threats it faces.

Oceans regulate our climate, support marine life, and sustain millions of people. But pollution threatens these ecosystems every day.

Our Mission

We believe that more than any technological innovation, spreading awareness and educational knowledge has a larger and broader impact.

By teaching people how pollution works and how to reduce it, we can create a real and long-lasting impact.

🧪 Interactive Experience

Pollution Simulator

Select a pollutant and click on the ocean to drop it. Watch how the water quality degrades, fish lose color, and the ecosystem collapses as pollution increases.

Water Color Changes

Watch the ocean turn murky and brown as pollution increases, just like real contaminated waters.

Marine Life Fades

Fish lose their vibrant colors and become ghostly — representing real biodiversity loss.

Toxicity Varies

Different pollutants have different toxicity levels. Oil and chemicals are far worse than plastic.
